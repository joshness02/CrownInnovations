Calf Pacifier

Calf Pacifiers are here to help you keep your bottle calves happy and healthy. Studies have shown that giving a calf 10-15 minutes of non-nutritive sucking after feeding can help reduce cross-sucking (calves sucking on each other) by up to 75 percent! This greatly reduces the spread of sickness.

Our mission is to provide safe and affordable help to farmers.
