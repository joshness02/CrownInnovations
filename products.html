Calf Pacifiers are here to help you keep your bottle calves happy and healthy! Now I know what your thinking: What in the world is a calf pacifier? The Calf Pacifier is just that, a pacifier for bottle calves. Many times, farmers will have many bottle calves, and they need to be housed somewhere. While the cheapest option is to pen them all together, this doesn't always work. Calves will suck on basically everything: fences, fingers, pants etc., but especially other calves. This is known as cross sucking, and can cause bald spots and sores, and can spread sicknesses as well.
Studies have shown that calves need extra non-nutritive sucking time after feeding to help them feel full. Our Calf Pacifier can help with that by giving the calves that extra sucking time.
All of our Calf Pacifiers have a sealed nipple, to prevent indigestion from calves sucking air.
They are also great to keep your calves occupied while you are feeding other calves.
